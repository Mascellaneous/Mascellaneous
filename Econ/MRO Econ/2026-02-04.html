The Question (題目)

下表顯示某經濟的不同日期的資產負債表。
The table below shows the balance sheet of an economy on different dates.

2025年1月1日 (Jan 1, 2025):

資產 Assets ($) 負債 Liabilities ($)
儲備 Reserves 400 存款 Deposits 1,000
貸款 Loans 600

2026年1月1日 (Jan 1, 2026):

資產 Assets ($) 負債 Liabilities ($)
儲備 Reserves 500 存款 Deposits 1,250
貸款 Loans 750

下列哪項／些可以解釋以上數據？
Which of the following can explain the above data?

  1. 商業銀行持有較少超額儲備。
    Commercial banks hold fewer excess reserves.
  2. 中央銀行下調法定儲備比率。
    The central bank lowers the required reserve ratio.
  3. 中央銀行在2025年向公眾購買政府債券。
    The central bank bought government bonds from the public in 2025.

A. 只有 (3) (Only 3)
B. 只有 (1)、(2) (Only 1 & 2)
C. 只有 (1)、(3) (Only 1 & 3)
D. 只有 (2)、(3) (Only 2 & 3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 實際儲備比率 (Actual Reserve Ratio, ARR)

這是銀行實際持有的儲備佔存款的百分比。

This is the percentage of deposits that banks actually hold as reserves.
$$ \text{ARR} = \frac{\text{Reserves (儲備)}}{\text{Deposits (存款)}} \times 100\% $$

2. 貨幣基礎與貨幣供應 (Monetary Base & Money Supply)

儲備 (Reserves): 屬於貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base) 的一部分。當央行向公眾買債，公眾將款項存入銀行，儲備會增加。

Reserves are part of the Monetary Base. When the Central Bank buys bonds from the public, and the public deposits the money, reserves increase.
The Answer (答案)

正確答案 (Correct Answer): A

我們使用「邏輯推演法」來找出答案，而非盲目猜測。

We use "Logical Deduction" to find the answer, rather than guessing blindly.
Step 1

計算實際儲備比率 (Calculate the Actual Reserve Ratio)

這類題目的關鍵在於檢查銀行體系的「流動性緊縮程度」是否改變。

The key to this type of question is to check if the "liquidity tightness" of the banking system has changed.
$$ 2025: \frac{400}{1000} = 40\% $$
$$ 2026: \frac{500}{1250} = 40\% $$

發現：比率維持 40% 不變。

Finding: The ratio remained constant at 40%.
Step 2

利用比率排除選項 (Eliminate Options using the Ratio)

既然實際儲備比率 (ARR) 維持不變，我們可以排除任何會改變 ARR 的選項。

Since the Actual Reserve Ratio (ARR) remained constant, we can eliminate any options that would change the ARR.
  • 選項 (1) 錯誤： 如果銀行持有較少超額儲備，ARR 應該會下降。
    Option (1) Incorrect: If banks hold fewer excess reserves, the ARR should decrease.
  • 選項 (2) 錯誤： 如果央行下調法定儲備比率，銀行會借出更多資金，導致存款增長快於儲備，ARR 應該會下降。
    Option (2) Incorrect: If the central bank lowers the required reserve ratio, banks lend more, causing deposits to grow faster than reserves, so ARR should decrease.
Step 3

尋找儲備變化的原因 (Identify the Cause of Reserve Change)

我們看到儲備由 $400 增加至 $500。這代表有新資金注入銀行體系。

We see Reserves increased from $400 to $500. This means new funds were injected into the banking system.
  • 選項 (3) 正確： 央行向公眾購買債券 $\rightarrow$ 公眾收到支票並存入銀行 $\rightarrow$ 銀行儲備增加。
    Option (3) Correct: Central Bank buys bonds from public $\rightarrow$ Public receives checks and deposits them $\rightarrow$ Bank reserves increase.
Step 4

驗證數字 (Verify the Numbers)

假設法定儲備比率維持 40%，當儲備增加 $100 (500-400)，存款會增加多少？

Assuming RRR stays at 40%, when reserves increase by $100, how much do deposits increase?
$$ \Delta D = \frac{\Delta R}{40\%} = \frac{100}{0.4} = 250 $$

數據顯示存款確實增加了 $250 (1250-1000)。計算吻合！

The data shows deposits indeed increased by $250. The calculation matches!

結論 (Conclusion)

由於只有選項 (3) 能解釋儲備的增加，且符合數據的變化，而選項 (1) 和 (2) 均與不變的儲備比率相矛盾。

因此，答案是 A。

Since only option (3) explains the increase in reserves and fits the data changes, while options (1) and (2) contradict the constant reserve ratio.
Therefore, the answer is A.
Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

考試技巧 (Exam Tips)

在處理資產負債表的題目時，請緊記以下順序：

When dealing with balance sheet questions, remember this order:
  1. 先算比率 (Calculate Ratio First): 這是最快排除錯誤選項的方法。 (Fastest way to eliminate wrong options)
  2. 看絕對值變化 (Look at Absolute Changes): 儲備增加了還是減少了？這決定了是擴張性還是緊縮性政策。 (Did reserves go up or down? This determines if it's expansionary or contractionary.)
  3. 驗證乘數效應 (Verify Multiplier): 確保 $\Delta D$ 與 $\Delta R$ 的比例符合計算出的比率。 (Ensure the ratio of change in deposits to change in reserves matches the calculated ratio.)
