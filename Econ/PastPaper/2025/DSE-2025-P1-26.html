經濟學概念解析：生產鏈與 GDP 計算

Economics Concept Analysis: Production Chain and GDP Calculation

課題 (Topic): 國民收入會計 (National Income Accounting) - 增值法與要素成本 (Value Added & Factor Cost)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆

題目 (The Question)

下圖顯示某經濟中咖啡豆研磨機的生產鏈。

The following diagram shows a production chain of coffee grinders in an economy.

數據摘要 (Data Summary):

  • 外國出口商 (Foreign exporters) $\rightarrow$ 本地零售商 (Local retailers): $90
  • 本地材料 (Local materials): $70 $\rightarrow$ 本地生產商 (Local manufacturers)
  • 本地生產商 (Local manufacturers) $\rightarrow$ 本地零售商 (Local retailers): $140
  • 本地生產商 (Local manufacturers) $\rightarrow$ 外國零售商 (Foreign retailers): $80
  • 本地零售商 (Local retailers) $\rightarrow$ 本地消費者 (Local consumers): $440 (包含 $60 銷售稅 / sales tax inclusive)

以上生產鏈對該經濟本地生產總值（以要素成本計算）的貢獻是多少？

What is the contribution of the above production chain to the economy's GDP at factor cost?
  • A. $370
  • B. $430
  • C. $460
  • D. $490

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 增值法 (Value Added Method)

本地生產總值 (GDP) 是所有常住生產單位的增值總和。增值的計算方法是將產出總值減去中間消耗。

GDP is the sum of value added of all resident producing units. Value added is calculated by subtracting intermediate consumption from gross output.
$$ \text{增值 (Value Added)} = \text{產出價值 (Gross Output)} - \text{中間消耗 (Intermediate Consumption)} $$

2. 市價與要素成本 (Market Price vs. Factor Cost)

市價 (Market Price) 包含了間接稅（如銷售稅），而要素成本 (Factor Cost) 則扣除了間接稅並加上津貼。

Market price includes indirect taxes (like sales tax), while factor cost excludes indirect taxes and includes subsidies.
$$ \text{GDP}_{\text{fc}} = \text{GDP}_{\text{mp}} - \text{間接稅 (Indirect Taxes)} + \text{津貼 (Subsidies)} $$

3. 進口與出口 (Imports & Exports)

計算 GDP 時，必須扣除進口成分（因為這不是本地生產），並包含出口（這是本地生產但賣給外國）。

When calculating GDP, imports must be deducted (as they are not locally produced), and exports must be included (as they are locally produced but sold abroad).

答案解析 (Answer Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Answer): A ($370)

解題方法 (Solution Method)

我們可以使用支出面法 (Expenditure Approach) 或 增值法 (Value Added Approach) 來計算。這裡用支出面法驗證最為快速，再用增值法詳細拆解。

We can use either the Expenditure Approach or the Value Added Approach. The Expenditure Approach is faster for verification, while the Value Added Approach provides a detailed breakdown.

方法一：支出面法 (Method 1: Expenditure Approach)

公式：\( \text{GDP}_{\text{mp}} = C + I + G + (X - M) \)

  • C (私人消費開支): 本地消費者支付的金額 = $440
  • X (出口): 本地生產商賣給外國零售商 = $80
  • M (進口): 外國出口商賣給本地零售商 = $90
$$ \text{GDP}_{\text{mp}} = 440 + 80 - 90 = 430 $$

題目要求以要素成本計算 (at factor cost)，所以需要扣除銷售稅。

The question asks for GDP at factor cost, so we need to deduct the sales tax.
$$ \text{GDP}_{\text{fc}} = \text{GDP}_{\text{mp}} - \text{間接稅 (Indirect Tax)} $$ $$ \text{GDP}_{\text{fc}} = 430 - 60 = 370 $$

方法二：增值法 (Method 2: Value Added Approach)

計算每個本地生產階段的增值：

1. 本地材料供應商 (Local Materials Supplier):

  • 產出 (Output): $70
  • 投入 (Input): $0 (假設)
  • 增值 (VA): $70

2. 本地生產商 (Local Manufacturers):

  • 產出 (Output): $140 (賣給本地) + $80 (賣給外國) = $220
  • 投入 (Input): $70 (本地材料)
  • 增值 (VA): $220 - $70 = $150

3. 本地零售商 (Local Retailers):

  • 產出 (Output): $440 (市價)
  • 投入 (Input): $140 (本地產品) + $90 (外國進口) = $230
  • 增值 (VA at market price): $440 - $230 = $210

總和 (Total):

$$ \text{GDP}_{\text{mp}} = 70 + 150 + 210 = 430 $$

最後轉換為要素成本：

$$ \text{GDP}_{\text{fc}} = 430 - 60 = 370 $$

題目拆解與陷阱 (Problem Decomposition & Traps)

這類題目主要測試學生是否能區分「中間消耗」、「進口」以及「稅項」。

This type of question tests whether students can distinguish between "intermediate consumption", "imports", and "taxes".
  • 陷阱 1：忽略進口扣除 (Ignoring Import Deduction)
    有些同學會直接將最終銷售額 $440 加上出口 $80，得出 $520，這是錯誤的，因為 $440 中包含了 $90 的進口貨值，這部分不是本地生產的。 Some students might simply add final sales $440 and exports $80 to get $520. This is wrong because the $440 includes $90 of import value, which is not locally produced.
  • 陷阱 2：忘記扣除稅項 (Forgetting Tax Deduction)
    題目明確要求「以要素成本計算」(at factor cost)。如果不扣除 $60 的銷售稅，就會算出 $430 (選項 B)，這是市價計算的 GDP。 The question explicitly asks for "at factor cost". If you don't deduct the $60 sales tax, you get $430 (Option B), which is GDP at market price.
  • 陷阱 3：重複計算 (Double Counting)
    如果你將 $70 (材料) + $140 (批發) + $440 (零售) 相加，你就犯了嚴重的重複計算錯誤。必須只計算「增值」部分。 If you add $70 (materials) + $140 (wholesale) + $440 (retail), you are committing a serious double counting error. You must only count the "value added".
