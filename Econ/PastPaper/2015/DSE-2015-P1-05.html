HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Market Structure / Cost (市場結構與成本) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Profit Maximization, MC, Fixed Cost

The Question (題目)

下表顯示在完全競爭市場內某廠商的邊際成本。 The schedule below shows the marginal cost of a firm in a perfectly competitive market.
Output / 產量 3456789
MC ($) / 邊際成本 55.566.5789.5
假設租用機器的租金(屬固定成本)上升了$2，而每單位產出的邊際成本減少了$0.5。該廠商利潤極大化的產量因此_________，而利潤 _________。 Suppose the rental of the machine hired, a fixed cost, increases by $2 while the marginal cost for each unit of the output is reduced by $0.5. As a result, the profit-maximising output of the firm _________ while the profit of the firm _________.
  • A. 上升 … 下跌 (increases … decreases)
  • B. 上升 … 可能上升或下跌 (increases … may increase or decrease)
  • C. 下跌 … 下跌 (decreases … decreases)
  • D. 下跌 … 可能上升或下跌 (decreases … may increase or decrease)
Concept Review (概念重溫)
1. 利潤極大化條件 (Profit Maximization Condition): Firms produce where Marginal Revenue equals Marginal Cost.
$$ MR = MC $$
2. 完全競爭市場 (Perfect Competition): The firm is a price taker, so Price equals Marginal Revenue.
$$ P = MR $$
3. 固定成本與邊際成本 (Fixed Cost vs. Marginal Cost): Fixed Cost (FC): Does not change with output. It does NOT affect Marginal Cost (MC). Changes in FC do not affect the profit-maximizing output level (unless the firm shuts down).
Marginal Cost (MC): The cost of producing one more unit. It determines the output level.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B (上升 … 可能上升或下跌)
第一步：分析產量 (Output) 的變化 Step 1: Analyze the change in Output
題目指出每單位產出的邊際成本 (MC) 減少了 $0.5。這代表 MC 曲線向下移動 (或向右移動)。 The question states that the MC for each unit is reduced by $0.5. This means the MC curve shifts downward (or to the right).
由於市場價格 (P) 不變 (完全競爭市場)，而廠商會在 \( P = MC \) 的水平生產。當 MC 下降，原本的價格現在會高於新的 MC，廠商會增加產量以獲取更多利潤，直到 \( P = New MC \)。因此，產量上升。 Since the market price (P) remains constant (perfect competition), and the firm produces where \( P = MC \). When MC falls, the original price is now higher than the new MC. The firm will increase output to capture more profit until \( P = New MC \). Therefore, output increases.
第二步：分析利潤 (Profit) 的變化 Step 2: Analyze the change in Profit
利潤受兩個因素影響： Profit is affected by two factors:
  • 壞消息：固定成本 (FC) 上升了 $2。這會直接減少利潤 $2。 Bad News: Fixed Cost (FC) increases by $2. This directly reduces profit by $2.
  • 好消息：邊際成本 (MC) 每一單位都減少 $0.5。這代表總可變成本 (TVC) 減少了 \( 0.5 \times Q \)。 Good News: Marginal Cost (MC) decreases by $0.5 for every unit. This means Total Variable Cost (TVC) decreases by \( 0.5 \times Q \).
淨影響 (Net Effect): 我們需要比較「成本節省」與「租金上升」的幅度。 Net Effect: We need to compare the magnitude of "cost savings" vs. "rent increase".
$$ \Delta \text{Profit} \approx (0.5 \times Q) - 2 $$
如果產量 \( Q \) 很小 (例如 3 單位)，節省只有 \( 3 \times 0.5 = 1.5 \)，少於租金上升的 2，利潤下跌。 If output \( Q \) is small (e.g., 3 units), the saving is only \( 3 \times 0.5 = 1.5 \), which is less than the rent increase of 2, so profit decreases.
如果產量 \( Q \) 很大 (例如 8 單位)，節省有 \( 8 \times 0.5 = 4 \)，多於租金上升的 2，利潤上升。 If output \( Q \) is large (e.g., 8 units), the saving is \( 8 \times 0.5 = 4 \), which is more than the rent increase of 2, so profit increases.
因為題目沒有提供市場價格，我們不知道具體的產量 \( Q \) 是多少。因此，利潤的變化是不確定的。 Since the question does not provide the market price, we do not know the specific output \( Q \). Therefore, the change in profit is uncertain.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目通常包含兩個變動，你需要將它們分開處理： This type of question usually involves two changes, and you need to handle them separately:
  1. 固定成本變動 (FC Change): 只影響利潤，不影響產量 (除非停業)。 Fixed Cost Change: Only affects profit, does NOT affect output (unless shutdown occurs).
  2. 邊際成本變動 (MC Change): 同時影響產量和利潤。 Marginal Cost Change: Affects both output and profit.
關鍵判斷點 (Pivot Point): Pivot Point:
利潤變化的不確定性來自於我們不知道 \( Q \) 的數值。如果題目給了具體的價格 (例如 P=$8)，我們就能算出 Q，從而算出確定的利潤變化。沒有 P，就沒有確定的 Q。 The uncertainty in profit change stems from not knowing the value of \( Q \). If the question gave a specific price (e.g., P=$8), we could calculate Q and thus the exact profit change. Without P, there is no specific Q.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：以為成本上升，產量一定減少。 Trap 1: Thinking that if costs rise, output must decrease.
糾正：只有邊際成本 (MC) 的上升才會導致產量減少。固定成本 (FC) 的上升不會改變 \( MC = MR \) 的交點。 Correction: Only an increase in Marginal Cost (MC) leads to a decrease in output. An increase in Fixed Cost (FC) does not change the intersection of \( MC = MR \).
陷阱 2：忽略了產量基數對成本節省的影響。 Trap 2: Ignoring the effect of the output base on cost savings.
糾正：MC 每單位減 $0.5，總節省是 \( 0.5 \times Q \)。很多學生只看到 $0.5 < $2 (租金)，就斷定利潤下跌，卻忘記了 $0.5 是「每單位」計算的。 Correction: MC drops by $0.5 per unit, so total savings is \( 0.5 \times Q \). Many students just see $0.5 < $2 (rent) and conclude profit falls, forgetting that $0.5 is applied "per unit".
Exam Tricks (考試技巧)
快速排除法 (Elimination Method): Elimination Method:
  1. 看到 MC 下降 $\rightarrow$ 供應增加 $\rightarrow$ 產量上升。立即排除 C 和 D。 See MC decreases $\rightarrow$ Supply increases $\rightarrow$ Output increases. Immediately eliminate C and D.
  2. 剩下 A 和 B。問自己：「我有足夠資料計算總成本變動嗎？」 Left with A and B. Ask yourself: "Do I have enough info to calculate the total cost change?"
  3. 資料不足 (不知道 Q) $\rightarrow$ 選擇「可能上升或下跌」。 Insufficient data (don't know Q) $\rightarrow$ Choose "may increase or decrease".
