HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Market Intervention (Taxation) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Tax Incidence, Elasticity, Equilibrium Price
The Question (題目)

[Traditional Chinese]
菠蘿原初的均衡價格為$5。假設政府對菠蘿開徵$4的從量稅。若菠蘿的需求彈性 __________ 其供應彈性，新的均衡價格可以是 __________。

[English]
The initial equilibrium price of pineapples is $5. Suppose the government imposes a per unit tax of $4 on pineapples. If the elasticity of demand for pineapples is __________ than their elasticity of supply, the new equilibrium price can be __________.

(1) 高於 higher …… $6
(2) 高於 higher …… $8
(3) 低於 lower …… $7
(4) 低於 lower …… $8
A. 只有 (1)、(3) only
B. 只有 (1)、(4) only
C. 只有 (2)、(3) only
D. 只有 (2)、(4) only
Concept Review (概念重溫)

稅項負擔與彈性 (Tax Burden and Elasticity)

當政府徵收從量稅 (Per Unit Tax) 時，稅項負擔會由消費者 (Consumer) 和生產者 (Producer) 共同分擔。分擔的比例取決於需求彈性 (\(E_d\)) 與 供應彈性 (\(E_s\)) 的相對大小。
When the government imposes a per unit tax, the tax burden is shared between consumers and producers. The share of the burden depends on the relative magnitude of Price Elasticity of Demand (\(E_d\)) and Price Elasticity of Supply (\(E_s\)).

核心定律 (The Golden Rule):
彈性較低的一方，議價能力較低，需承擔較多的稅項。
The side with lower elasticity has lower bargaining power and bears a larger share of the tax.

  • 若 \(E_d > E_s\)：消費者彈性較高（較敏感），消費者負擔 < 生產者負擔。
    If \(E_d > E_s\): Consumers are more elastic (sensitive), Consumer Burden < Producer Burden.
  • 若 \(E_d < E_s\)：消費者彈性較低（較依賴），消費者負擔 > 生產者負擔。
    If \(E_d < E_s\): Consumers are less elastic (dependent), Consumer Burden > Producer Burden.

價格公式 (Price Formula):
$$ \text{New Price } (P_1) = \text{Original Price } (P_0) + \text{Consumer's Tax Burden} $$ $$ P_1 = \$5 + \text{Consumer's Share} $$

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: B

已知條件 (Given):
1. 原價 (Original Price) \(P_0 = \$5\)
2. 稅款 (Tax) \(t = \$4\)
3. 稅款的一半 (Half of Tax) \(= \$2\)
4. 價格中位數 (Midpoint Price) \(= \$5 + \$2 = \$7\)

分析陳述 (1)：若需求彈性「高於」供應彈性，新價格是 $6

當 \(E_d > E_s\) (高於)，消費者較容易逃避稅項，因此消費者承擔的稅款會少於一半 (少於 $2)。
新價格 \(P_1\) 會在 $5 與 $7 之間。
\(\$5 < P_1 < \$7\)
$6 在此範圍內，故陳述正確。

When \(E_d > E_s\) (higher), consumers can escape the tax more easily, so they bear less than half of the tax (less than $2).
The new price \(P_1\) will be between $5 and $7.
\(\$5 < P_1 < \$7\)
$6 is within this range, so the statement is correct.
分析陳述 (2)：若需求彈性「高於」供應彈性，新價格是 $8

承上，若 \(E_d > E_s\)，消費者負擔少於 $2，新價格必須低於 $7。
$8 超出了範圍，故陳述錯誤。

As above, if \(E_d > E_s\), the consumer burden is less than $2, so the new price must be lower than $7.
$8 is outside this range, so the statement is incorrect.
分析陳述 (3)：若需求彈性「低於」供應彈性，新價格是 $7

當 \(E_d < E_s\) (低於)，消費者較難逃避，需承擔多於一半的稅款 (多於 $2)。
新價格 \(P_1\) 必須大於 $7。
\(\$7 < P_1 < \$9\) (上限是全數轉嫁的 $9)
$7 代表負擔剛好一半 (即 \(E_d = E_s\))，題目指明「低於」，故價格不能剛好是 $7。陳述錯誤。

When \(E_d < E_s\) (lower), consumers bear more than half of the tax (more than $2).
The new price \(P_1\) must be greater than $7.
\(\$7 < P_1 < \$9\) (Upper limit is full shifting $9)
$7 implies an exactly equal split (\(E_d = E_s\)). Since the question specifies "lower", the price cannot be exactly $7. The statement is incorrect.
分析陳述 (4)：若需求彈性「低於」供應彈性，新價格是 $8

承上，若 \(E_d < E_s\)，消費者負擔多於 $2，新價格範圍是 $7 至 $9。
$8 在此範圍內，故陳述正確。

As above, if \(E_d < E_s\), the consumer burden is more than $2, and the new price range is $7 to $9.
$8 is within this range, so the statement is correct.

結論： 只有 (1) 和 (4) 正確。選項 B 是答案。

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵點 (Pivot Point)

這類題目的關鍵在於找出「價格中位數」(Price Midpoint)。
The key to solving this type of question is finding the "Price Midpoint".

  1. 計算中位數： 原價 + (稅款 / 2) = \(5 + (4/2) = \$7\)。
    Calculate Midpoint: Original Price + (Tax / 2) = \(5 + 2 = \$7\).
  2. 判斷方向：
    • 消費者贏 (彈性高 \(E_d > E_s\)) \(\rightarrow\) 價格低於 $7。
    • 消費者輸 (彈性低 \(E_d < E_s\)) \(\rightarrow\) 價格高於 $7。
    Determine Direction:
    • Consumers Win (High Elasticity \(E_d > E_s\)) \(\rightarrow\) Price < $7.
    • Consumers Lose (Low Elasticity \(E_d < E_s\)) \(\rightarrow\) Price > $7.
Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)
  • 錯誤觀念 1： 以為價格一定會上升 $4 (全數轉嫁)。
    Misconception 1: Thinking the price will always rise by the full $4 (full tax shifting).
    糾正： 除非 \(E_d = 0\) (完全無彈性) 或 \(E_s = \infty\) (完全彈性)，否則價格升幅一定小於 $4。
  • 錯誤觀念 2： 忽略了「低於」意味著不包括相等。
    Misconception 2: Ignoring that "lower" implies strict inequality (not equal).
    糾正： 陳述 (3) 的 $7 是分水嶺。若 \(E_d < E_s\)，價格必須嚴格大於 $7。
