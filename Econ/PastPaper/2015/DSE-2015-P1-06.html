概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 勞工生產力 (Labour Productivity)
勞工生產力通常以「每工時平均產量」來量度。它反映了勞工的效率。 Labour productivity is usually measured by "average output per man-hour". It reflects the efficiency of labour.
$$ \text{勞工生產力} = \frac{\text{總產出}}{\text{總工時}} $$ $$ \text{Labour Productivity} = \frac{\text{Total Output}}{\text{Total Man-hours}} $$
2. 影響勞工生產力的因素 (Factors affecting Labour Productivity)
  • 人力資本 (Human Capital): 教育、訓練、健康、工作態度。
    Education, training, health, working attitude.
  • 資本物品的數量與質素 (Quantity and Quality of Capital): 機器越先進，工人生產力越高。
    More advanced machinery leads to higher worker productivity.
  • 生產技術 (Technology): 更佳的技術。
    Better technology.
  • 管理 (Management): 分工、激勵制度。
    Division of labour, incentive systems.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

第一步：計算勞工生產力 (Step 1: Calculate Labour Productivity)

在回答「為什麼有差異」之前，我們必須先算出兩間工廠的實際生產力，看看誰高誰低。

Before answering "why there is a difference", we must first calculate the actual productivity of both factories to see which one is higher.

$$ \text{總工時 (Total Man-hours)} = \text{工人人數 (Workers)} \times \text{平均工時 (Avg Hours)} $$

工廠甲 (Factory A):

  • 總工時 = \( 25 \times 8 = 200 \) 小時 (hours)
  • 勞工生產力 = \( \frac{12,000}{200} = \mathbf{60} \) 單位/小時 (units/hour)

工廠乙 (Factory B):

  • 總工時 = \( 50 \times 6 = 300 \) 小時 (hours)
  • 勞工生產力 = \( \frac{15,000}{300} = \mathbf{50} \) 單位/小時 (units/hour)

結論：工廠甲的生產力 (60) 高於 工廠乙 (50)。

Conclusion: Productivity of Factory A (60) is higher than Factory B (50).

第二步：尋找原因 (Step 2: Find the Reason)

題目問的是差異的原因。既然我們知道甲比乙好，我們需要找一個選項能解釋「為什麼甲的效率較高」或者「為什麼乙的效率較低」。

The question asks for the reason for the difference. Since we know A is better than B, we need to find an option that explains "why A is more efficient" or "why B is less efficient".

答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案： B (Correct Option: B)

A. 工廠甲以時薪支付工資，工廠乙則以件工計算。
Factory A uses time rate while Factory B uses piece rate to pay its workers.

分析： 一般而言，件薪制（Piece rate）更能提升工人的生產動機，從而提高生產力；而計時制（Time rate）可能導致工人怠工。如果甲用計時，乙用件薪，理論上乙的生產力應該較高。這與我們的計算結果（甲 > 乙）相反。
Analysis: Generally, piece rate motivates workers to produce more, increasing productivity, while time rate might lead to shirking. If A uses time rate and B uses piece rate, theoretically B's productivity should be higher. This contradicts our calculation (A > B).

B. 工廠甲向工人提供較多的訓練。
Factory A provides more training for its workers.

分析： 訓練能提升人力資本，增加工人的技能和效率。如果甲提供較多訓練，甲的工人生產力理應較高。這與我們的計算結果（甲 > 乙）吻合。
Analysis: Training improves human capital, increasing workers' skills and efficiency. If A provides more training, A's workers should be more productive. This is consistent with our calculation (A > B).

C. 工廠乙聘請的兼職工人有較高技術。
The part-time workers employed by Factory B are more skilled.

分析： 技術較高的工人通常生產力較高。如果乙的工人技術較高，乙的生產力應該比甲高。這與我們的計算結果（甲 > 乙）相反。
Analysis: Higher-skilled workers usually have higher productivity. If B's workers are more skilled, B's productivity should be higher than A's. This contradicts our calculation (A > B).

D. 工廠乙運用較多資本物品。
Factory B uses more capital goods.

分析： 資本物品（如機器）能協助工人提升產量。如果乙運用較多資本，乙的勞工生產力應該較高。這與我們的計算結果（甲 > 乙）相反。
Analysis: Capital goods (like machinery) assist workers in increasing output. If B uses more capital, B's labour productivity should be higher. This contradicts our calculation (A > B).

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

1. 混淆「總產出」與「生產力」 (Confusing Total Output with Productivity)
學生常看到工廠乙的總產出 (15,000) 高於工廠甲 (12,000)，就誤以為乙的生產力較高，從而選擇了 C 或 D。這是錯誤的！必須除以總工時。
Students often see that Factory B's total output (15,000) is higher than Factory A's (12,000) and mistakenly assume B has higher productivity, leading them to choose C or D. This is wrong! You must divide by total man-hours.
2. 忽略計算步驟 (Skipping the Calculation)
這類題目不能單憑直覺。如果不先算出 60 和 50 這兩個數字，你根本無法判斷哪個選項是正確的解釋。
You cannot rely on intuition for this type of question. If you don't calculate the numbers 60 and 50 first, you simply cannot judge which option is the correct explanation.

考試技巧 (Exam Tricks)

「先算後找」 (Calculate First, Match Later)
遇到這類數據表題目：

  1. 算： 快速計算兩者的每小時產量 (A=60, B=50)。
  2. 定： 確定方向 -> A 比 B 強。
  3. 找： 掃描選項，找出「對 A 有利」或「對 B 不利」的因素。
When facing this type of data table question:
  1. Calculate: Quickly calculate the output per hour for both (A=60, B=50).
  2. Determine: Set the direction -> A is better than B.
  3. Match: Scan the options to find a factor that is "favourable to A" or "unfavourable to B".
