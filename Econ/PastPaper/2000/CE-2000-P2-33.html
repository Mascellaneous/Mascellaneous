The Question (題目)

下列哪一項包括入香港本地生產總值的計算之內？
Which of the following is included in the calculation of the gross domestic product (GDP) of Hong Kong?
  • A. 香港某業主自住摟宇的估計租值
    the estimated rental value of an owner-occupied property in Hong Kong.
  • B. 政府給予香港的大學生的低息貸款
    the low interest government loans to university students in Hong Kong.
  • C. 支持予外國投資者所持有外匯基金票據的利息
    the interest paid to foreign investors holding Exchange Fund bills.
  • D. 香港某銀行所發行股票價格的增值
    the increase in the price of the shares issued by a bank in Hong Kong.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

GDP Definition (本地生產總值定義) 本地生產總值是指一個國家或地區在一段特定期間內（例如一年），所有常住生產單位生產的最終物品和服務的市場總值。 GDP is the total market value of all final goods and services produced by resident producing units in a country or territory within a specified period (e.g., one year).
Production Boundary (生產界限) GDP 旨在量度「生產」。因此，純粹的資產轉移、金融交易或二手買賣（不涉及新生產）均不計算在內。 GDP measures "production". Therefore, pure asset transfers, financial transactions, or second-hand sales (involving no new production) are NOT included.
Imputed Value (設算價值) 某些沒有經過市場交易但涉及生產的項目，需要估算其價值計入 GDP。最常見的例子是自住樓宇的設算租金。 Some items involving production but no market transaction must have their value estimated (imputed) to be included in GDP. The most common example is imputed rent for owner-occupied housing.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A
A Correct (正確)

分析：雖然業主自住沒有實際支付租金，但該樓宇提供了「居住服務」。統計處會按市場租值估算這部分服務的價值（設算租金），並計入 GDP 的私人消費開支中。 Analysis: Although an owner-occupier does not pay actual rent, the property provides "housing services". The Census and Statistics Department estimates the value of these services (imputed rent) based on market rates and includes it in GDP under Private Consumption Expenditure.

B Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：貸款本身屬於金融資產的轉移（借貸），並不涉及當期的生產活動。這只是購買力的轉移，故不計入 GDP。 Analysis: Loans are transfers of financial assets (borrowing/lending) and do not involve current production activities. This is merely a transfer of purchasing power, so it is excluded from GDP.

C Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：政府（或外匯基金）支付的債券/票據利息，在香港的國民收入會計中通常被視為轉移支付，而非對生產要素的報酬，因此不計入 GDP。 Analysis: Interest paid on government (or Exchange Fund) bonds/bills is generally treated as a transfer payment in Hong Kong's national income accounting, not as payment for factor services, and is thus excluded from GDP.

D Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：股票價格上升屬於資本增值 (Capital Gain)。這只是資產價值的帳面變動，並沒有新的物品或服務被生產出來。 Analysis: An increase in stock prices represents a capital gain. This is merely a change in the book value of an asset; no new goods or services have been produced.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目主要測試考生能否分辨「生產活動」與「財富轉移」。解題關鍵在於問自己一個問題：
This type of question tests whether you can distinguish between "production activities" and "wealth transfers". The key is to ask yourself:

"Is there any new good or service produced in the current period?"
（本期內是否有新的物品或服務被生產出來？）
  • 選項 A (Pivot Point): 雖然沒有金錢交易，但「居住服務」確實被生產並享用了。這是唯一的例外（設算項目）。 Option A (Pivot Point): Even though no money changed hands, "housing services" were indeed produced and consumed. This is a key exception (imputed item).
  • 選項 B, C, D: 全都涉及金融資產或價格變動，沒有實質生產。 Options B, C, D: All involve financial assets or price changes, with no real production.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ Trap: "No Money = No GDP?" (沒有金錢交易就不計入 GDP？)

很多學生誤以為沒有金錢轉手的項目就不計入 GDP。這是錯誤的。設算租金 (Imputed Rent) 和 農民自給自足的農產品 都是典型的計入項目。 Many students mistakenly believe that if no money changes hands, it's not in GDP. This is wrong. Imputed Rent and farmers' self-consumed produce are classic examples of included items.

⚠️ Trap: Capital Gains (資本增值)

股票、樓市升值帶來的財富增加，雖然讓你「更有錢」，但這不是生產，所以不計入 GDP。只有股票經紀或地產代理的佣金 (Commission) 才計入 GDP。 Wealth increase from rising stock or property prices makes you "richer", but it is not production, so it is excluded from GDP. Only the commission earned by brokers or agents is included.
