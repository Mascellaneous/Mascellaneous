HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Market Intervention (Taxation) & Elasticity Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Tax Revenue, Price Elasticity of Supply

The Question (題目)

當物品X的從量銷售稅減半時，政府來自物品X的稅項收入亦減半。一個可能的解釋是 _____________。 When the per-unit sales tax on Good X is halved, the government's tax revenue from Good X is also halved. One possible explanation is that _____________.
  • A. 物品X的供應屬完全彈性供應
    A. the supply of Good X is perfectly elastic
  • B. 物品X的供應屬完全無彈性供應
    B. the supply of Good X is perfectly inelastic
  • C. 物品X的需求屬完全彈性需求
    C. the demand for Good X is perfectly elastic
  • D. 物品X的需求屬單一彈性需求
    D. the demand for Good X is unitarily elastic

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 稅項收入公式 (Tax Revenue Formula): Government Tax Revenue is calculated as the per-unit tax multiplied by the quantity transacted.

$$ \text{Tax Revenue (R)} = \text{Per-unit Tax (t)} \times \text{Quantity (Q)} $$

2. 彈性與交易量的關係 (Elasticity and Quantity): Normally, when tax decreases, the cost of production falls (or supply increases), leading to a decrease in price and an increase in quantity transacted. However, there are special cases:

  • 完全無彈性供應 (Perfectly Inelastic Supply): 供應曲線垂直。無論價格或稅項如何變動，交易量 ($Q$) 保持不變。 Perfectly Inelastic Supply: Vertical supply curve. Quantity ($Q$) remains constant regardless of price or tax changes.
  • 完全無彈性需求 (Perfectly Inelastic Demand): 需求曲線垂直。交易量 ($Q$) 保持不變。 Perfectly Inelastic Demand: Vertical demand curve. Quantity ($Q$) remains constant.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B

Step-by-Step Analysis (逐步分析)

邏輯推演 (Logical Deduction): Let's look at the math. We are given that tax ($t$) is halved and Revenue ($R$) is halved.

$$ t_{new} = 0.5 \times t_{old} $$ $$ R_{new} = 0.5 \times R_{old} $$ $$ \text{Since } R = t \times Q \Rightarrow (0.5 t_{old}) \times Q_{new} = 0.5 \times (t_{old} \times Q_{old}) $$ $$ \text{Divide both sides by } 0.5 t_{old}: $$ $$ Q_{new} = Q_{old} $$

這意味著，儘管稅項改變了，交易量 ($Q$) 必須保持不變。 This implies that despite the change in tax, the Quantity ($Q$) must remain constant.

選項 B (正確): 物品X的供應屬完全無彈性供應 Option B (Correct): The supply of Good X is perfectly inelastic.
當供應完全無彈性（垂直線）時，供應量固定不變。即使稅項減少，供應商願意提供的數量不變，因此 $Q$ 不變。根據公式 $R = t \times Q$，如果 $Q$ 不變，$t$ 減半，$R$ 自然減半。 When supply is perfectly inelastic (vertical line), the quantity supplied is fixed. Even if the tax decreases, the quantity provided by suppliers remains the same, so $Q$ is constant. According to $R = t \times Q$, if $Q$ is constant and $t$ is halved, $R$ is naturally halved.

選項 A & C (錯誤): 完全彈性 (水平線) Options A & C (Incorrect): Perfectly elastic (Horizontal lines).
如果是完全彈性供應或需求，稅項減少會導致價格變動，進而導致交易量 $Q$ 大幅增加。如果 $t$ 減半但 $Q$ 增加，稅收收入 $R$ 不會剛好減半（通常會多於一半）。 If supply or demand were perfectly elastic, a tax cut would cause a price change leading to a significant increase in quantity $Q$. If $t$ halves but $Q$ increases, Revenue $R$ would not be exactly halved (it would likely be more than half).

選項 D (錯誤): 單一彈性需求 Option D (Incorrect): Unitarily elastic demand.
單一彈性需求是指價格變動百分比等於需求量變動百分比，這導致「總支出」($P \times Q$) 不變，而不是稅收 ($t \times Q$) 的關係。這是一個干擾項。 Unitary elasticity means the percentage change in price equals the percentage change in quantity demanded, leading to constant "Total Expenditure" ($P \times Q$), not a specific relationship for Tax Revenue ($t \times Q$). This is a distractor.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵點 (The Pivot Point): The key is to mathematically isolate the variable $Q$.

  • 題目說：稅 ($t$) $\downarrow 50\%$，收入 ($R$) $\downarrow 50\%$。 Question states: Tax ($t$) $\downarrow 50\%$, Revenue ($R$) $\downarrow 50\%$.
  • 公式：$R = t \times Q$。 Formula: $R = t \times Q$.
  • 推論：只有當 $Q$ 完全不變 ($0\% \text{ change}$) 時，這條算式才成立。 Deduction: The equation only holds true if $Q$ remains completely unchanged ($0\% \text{ change}$).
  • 經濟學意義：什麼情況下 $Q$ 不受價格/稅項影響？只有垂直的曲線（完全無彈性）。 Economic meaning: When is $Q$ unaffected by price/tax? Only with a vertical curve (Perfectly Inelastic).

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：混淆「稅收收入」與「總支出/總收入」 Trap 1: Confusing "Tax Revenue" with "Total Expenditure/Total Revenue".
很多學生看到「收入...不變」或有規律的變化，就會聯想到選項 D (單一彈性)。但單一彈性是關於 $P \times Q$ (消費者支出)，而這題問的是 $t \times Q$ (政府稅收)。 Many students see patterns in "revenue" and immediately think of Option D (Unitary Elasticity). However, unitary elasticity relates to $P \times Q$ (Consumer Expenditure), whereas this question asks about $t \times Q$ (Government Tax Revenue).

陷阱 2：直覺認為減稅一定會增加交易量 Trap 2: Intuitively thinking tax cuts always increase quantity.
通常情況下是的，但在「完全無彈性」(垂直線) 的極端情況下，交易量是被供應限制死的，不會隨稅項變動。 Normally yes, but in the extreme case of "Perfectly Inelastic" (vertical line), quantity is constrained by supply and does not change with tax.

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

代入法 (Substitution Method): If you forget the theory, visualize the graphs:

  1. 畫一條垂直的供應線 (完全無彈性)。 Draw a vertical supply curve (Perfectly Inelastic).
  2. 想像稅項減少（供應線向下移，但因為是垂直的，所以看起來沒動，或者理解為生產者願意接受的價格降低）。 Imagine the tax decreases (Supply curve shifts down, but since it's vertical, the quantity line doesn't move).
  3. 交易量 $Q$ 沒變。 Quantity $Q$ stays the same.
  4. 稅率 $t$ 減半。 Tax rate $t$ halves.
  5. 結論：$t \times Q$ 肯定減半。符合題目！選 B。 Conclusion: $t \times Q$ must halve. Matches the question! Choose B.
