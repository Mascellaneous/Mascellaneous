常見誤解：看似生產，實為服務
Common Misconceptions: Looks like Production, Actually Services

這是一個非常好的問題！在 DSE 經濟學中，區分「第二級生產」（製造）與「第三級生產」（服務）的關鍵，往往在於產品是「標準化的大量生產」還是「個人化的服務」。
This is an excellent question! In HKDSE Economics, the key to distinguishing between "Secondary Production" (Manufacturing) and "Tertiary Production" (Services) often lies in whether the product is "standardized mass production" or a "personalized service".

1. 裁縫 / 度身訂造 (Tailoring) 👕
❌ 誤解：裁縫剪布、車衣，造出一件衣服，所以是第二級生產。
Misconception: Tailors cut fabric and sew to make clothes, so it's Secondary production.
✅ 正解：第三級生產 (Tertiary Production)
Correct: Tertiary Production
原因：裁縫提供的是個人化服務。雖然有實物產出，但顧客購買的是師傅的技術和量身定做的服務。相反，工廠大量生產成衣（Garment Factory）才是第二級生產。
Reason: Tailors provide a personalized service. Although there is a physical output, the customer is buying the skill and customization. In contrast, mass-producing clothes in a factory is Secondary production.
2. 維修業 (Repair Services) 🔧
❌ 誤解：修車師傅更換零件，把壞車變好車，是「再製造」，所以是第二級。
Misconception: Mechanics replace parts and fix cars, which is "re-manufacturing", so it's Secondary.
✅ 正解：第三級生產 (Tertiary Production)
Correct: Tertiary Production
原因：維修是為了恢復產品的功能，屬於支援服務。汽車製造廠（Car Manufacturer）是第二級，但車房（Garage）是第三級。
Reason: Repairing is about restoring functionality, which is a support service. A car manufacturer is Secondary, but a garage is Tertiary.
3. 出版業 (Publishing) 📚
❌ 誤解：出版社造書出來賣，書是實物，所以是第二級。
Misconception: Publishers make books to sell. Books are physical objects, so it's Secondary.
✅ 正解：第三級生產 (Tertiary Production)
Correct: Tertiary Production
原因：這裡要分得很細：
1. 印刷廠 (Printing Press): 負責印書、釘裝 ➝ 第二級 (Secondary)。
2. 出版社 (Publisher): 負責策劃、編輯、版權交易、發行 ➝ 第三級 (Tertiary)。
Reason: We must distinguish carefully:
1. Printing Press: Prints and binds books ➝ Secondary.
2. Publisher: Handles editing, copyright, distribution ➝ Tertiary.
4. 室內設計 (Interior Design) 🎨
❌ 誤解：設計師畫圖則，也是在「建造」房子的一部分。
Misconception: Designers draw plans, which is part of "building" the house.
✅ 正解：第三級生產 (Tertiary Production)
Correct: Tertiary Production
原因：建造業 (Construction) 本身是第二級生產（蓋樓宇、橋樑）。但單純提供設計意念或顧問服務的公司，屬於商業服務（第三級）。
Reason: Construction itself is Secondary production (building flats, bridges). However, companies providing only design concepts or consultancy are Business Services (Tertiary).
💡 考試黃金法則 (Golden Rule for Exams)
問自己：這個行業的主要產出是「標準化的實物」 (Standardized Good) 還是「無形的服務/體驗」 (Intangible Service/Experience)？
Ask yourself: Is the main output of this industry a "Standardized Good" or an "Intangible Service/Experience"?
