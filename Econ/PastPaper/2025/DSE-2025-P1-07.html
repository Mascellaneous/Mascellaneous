The Question (題目)

The table below shows the cost-output relationship of a firm in a perfectly competitive market. The fixed cost of the firm is $6 and the market price is $20.

產出 Output (Q) 1234567
總可變成本 TVC ($) 41224406084112

假設固定成本下降$4，而每單位產出的邊際成本上升$4。結果，該廠商的利潤會___________。

Suppose the fixed cost decreases by $4 and the marginal cost for each unit of output increases by $4. As a result, the firm's profits will _________.

  • A. 維持不變 (remain unchanged)
  • B. 下降$6 (decrease by $6)
  • C. 下降$12 (decrease by $12)
  • D. 下降$16 (decrease by $16)
Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 利潤極大化條件 (Profit Maximization Condition): 廠商會生產至邊際收益等於邊際成本的產量水平。

Firms produce at the output level where Marginal Revenue equals Marginal Cost.

$$ MR = MC $$

2. 完全競爭市場 (Perfect Competition): 廠商是受價者 (Price Taker)，因此邊際收益等於市場價格。

In perfect competition, the firm is a price taker, so Marginal Revenue equals the Market Price.

$$ P = MR $$

3. 邊際成本計算 (Calculating MC): 邊際成本是生產多一單位產品所增加的總成本（或總可變成本）。

Marginal Cost is the change in total cost (or total variable cost) when output increases by one unit.

$$ MC_n = TVC_n - TVC_{n-1} $$

4. 利潤公式 (Profit Formula):

$$ \text{Profit } (\pi) = TR - TC = (P \times Q) - (TVC + TFC) $$
The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
Correct Option: C

原本均衡產量 ($Q_1$) 為 5 單位。

Initial equilibrium output ($Q_1$) is 5 units.

原本總收入 ($TR_1$) = $20 \times 5 = 100$

原本總成本 ($TC_1$) = $TVC + TFC = 60 + 6 = 66$

原本利潤 ($\pi_1$) = $100 - 66 = \$34$

第二步：計算變動後的均衡產量與利潤

Step 2: Calculate the new equilibrium output and profit after changes.

變動 1： 固定成本 (FC) 下降 $4 $\rightarrow$ 新 FC = $6 - 4 = 2$。
變動 2： 邊際成本 (MC) 每單位上升 $4 $\rightarrow$ 新 MC = 舊 MC + 4。

Change 1: Fixed Cost (FC) decreases by $4 $\rightarrow$ New FC = $2$.

由於 $P=20$ 保持不變，新的均衡點出現在 $MC' = 20$ 時。

Since $P=20$ remains constant, the new equilibrium occurs where $MC' = 20$.

新均衡產量 ($Q_2$) 為 4 單位。

New equilibrium output ($Q_2$) is 4 units.

計算新成本：
在 Q=4 時，舊 TVC 為 40。
因為每單位 MC 上升 $4，生產 4 單位的總可變成本增加 $4 \times 4 = 16$。
新 TVC = $40 + 16 = 56$。
新 TC = 新 TVC + 新 FC = $56 + 2 = 58$。

Calculating New Cost:
At Q=4, old TVC was 40.
Since MC increased by $4 per unit, TVC for 4 units increases by $4 \times 4 = 16$.
New TVC = $40 + 16 = 56$.
New TC = New TVC + New FC = $56 + 2 = 58$.

新總收入 ($TR_2$) = $20 \times 4 = 80$

新利潤 ($\pi_2$) = $80 - 58 = \$22$

第三步：比較利潤變化

Step 3: Compare the change in profit.

$$ \Delta \pi = \pi_2 - \pi_1 = 22 - 34 = -12 $$

利潤下降了 $12。故答案為 C。

Profit decreases by $12. Therefore, the answer is C.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵轉折點 (The Pivot Point):
學生必須意識到產量 (Q) 會改變。MC 的上升會導致最優產量減少。如果你假設產量維持在 5 單位，你就會計算錯誤。

The Pivot Point:
Students must realize that Output (Q) will change. The increase in MC causes the optimal output level to drop. If you assume output stays at 5 units, you will miscalculate.

題目陷阱 (Distractors):
固定成本 (FC) 的變動只影響利潤，不影響 MC，因此不影響產量決策。但 MC 的變動同時影響產量和成本。

Distractors:
Changes in Fixed Cost (FC) only affect profit, not MC, and thus do not affect the output decision. However, changes in MC affect both output and costs.

Common Mistakes (常犯錯誤)
  • 錯誤 1： 忘記重新尋找 $MR=MC$ 的產量，直接用 Q=5 計算新利潤。
  • 錯誤 2： 計算新 TVC 時出錯。有些人只把總成本加 $4，而忘記是「每單位」MC 都加 $4 (所以在 Q=4 時，TVC 增加了 $4 \times 4$)。
  • 錯誤 3： 忽略固定成本下降的 $4。
  • Mistake 1: Forgetting to re-evaluate the $MR=MC$ condition and calculating new profit using the old output Q=5.
  • Mistake 2: Incorrectly calculating the new TVC. Some might just add $4 to the total cost, forgetting it is an increase per unit (so at Q=4, TVC increases by $4 \times 4$).
  • Mistake 3: Ignoring the $4 decrease in fixed costs.
Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

系統化列表法 (Systematic Tabulation):

不要試圖用心算解決這類雙重變動 (Double Shift) 的題目。在試卷空白處快速畫出兩列：舊 MC 和 新 MC。這只需 30 秒，但能保證準確率 100%。

Systematic Tabulation:
Do not try to solve "Double Shift" problems with mental math. Quickly scribble two columns on your exam paper: Old MC and New MC. It takes 30 seconds but guarantees 100% accuracy.
