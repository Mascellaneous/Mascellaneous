HKDSE Economics Tutor / 經濟學導師解析

主題 (Topic): 產權與交易費用 (Property Rights and Transaction Costs)

題型 (Question Type): 短題目 (Short Question)

難度 (Difficulty): ★☆☆☆☆ (1/5)

關鍵概念 (Key Concepts): 私有產權 (Private Property Rights), 獨享使用權 (Exclusive Right to Use), 獨享收入權 (Exclusive Right to Derive Income), 轉讓權 (Right to Transfer)

1. 題目 (The Question)

寫出私有產權的定義。
Give the definition of the private property rights.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

私有產權 (Private Property Rights) 是市場經濟運作的基石。它界定了資源的擁有者可以如何處置其資產。完整的私有產權必須包含三個不可或缺的權利：

Private Property Rights are the cornerstone of a market economy. They define how the owner of a resource can dispose of their assets. Complete private property rights must include three indispensable rights:
  • 獨享使用權 (Exclusive right to use): 決定資產如何被使用的權利。
  • 獨享收入權 (Exclusive right to derive income): 從資產中獲取經濟利益的權利。
  • 轉讓權 (Right to transfer): 將資產的擁有權（全部或部分）賣給或贈予他人的權利。

簡單來說，產權的方程式可以表示為：

Simply put, the equation for property rights can be expressed as:

$$ \text{Private Property Rights} = \text{Exclusive Use} + \text{Exclusive Income} + \text{Transferability} $$

3. 建議答案與詳細分析 (Suggested Answer & Detailed Analysis)

私有產權是指擁有者對其資產擁有以下三種權利：

Private property rights refer to the owner having the following three rights over their assets:
  1. 獨享使用權
    Exclusive right to use
  2. 獨享收入權
    Exclusive right to derive income
  3. 轉讓權
    Right to transfer

⚠️ 重點解析：是否必須寫上「獨享」(Exclusive)？
Crucial Analysis: Must we write the word "Exclusive"?

是的，對於「使用權」和「收入權」，必須寫上「獨享」(Exclusive) 或「排他性」。

Yes, for the "right to use" and "right to derive income", you MUST write the word "exclusive".

詳細解釋：
在經濟學中，「私有」(Private) 的核心本質在於排他性 (Excludability)。如果只有「使用權」而沒有「獨享使用權」，這意味著其他人也可以使用該資產（例如公海裡的魚或公共公園），這就變成了「公共產權」(Common Property) 而非私有產權。同樣地，如果你不能「獨享」資產帶來的收入，這項資產的市場價值就會大打折扣。
然而，對於「轉讓權」(Right to transfer)，通常不需要加上「獨享」二字。因為轉讓權本身就是將你所擁有的權利交給另一個人，權利本身已隱含了對資產擁有權的處置。

Detailed Explanation:
In economics, the core essence of "Private" lies in Excludability. If you only have the "right to use" without the "exclusive right to use", it means others can also use the asset (like fish in the open sea or a public park). This makes it "Common Property" rather than private property. Similarly, if you cannot "exclusively" derive income from the asset, its market value diminishes significantly.
However, for the "right to transfer", the word "exclusive" is usually not required. This is because the right to transfer inherently means handing over your rights to someone else; the right itself implies the disposal of asset ownership.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 漏寫「獨享」(Exclusive)： 這是最致命的錯誤。只寫「使用權」和「收入權」在 DSE 考試中通常會被扣分或不給分。
    Omitting "Exclusive": This is the most fatal error. Writing only "right to use" and "right to income" will usually result in mark deduction or zero marks in the DSE exam.
  • 將「轉讓權」寫成「買賣權」： 轉讓 (Transfer) 包括了買賣、贈予、甚至遺產繼承。買賣只是轉讓的其中一種形式，因此寫「轉讓權」才最準確。
    Writing "right to buy/sell" instead of "right to transfer": Transfer includes buying/selling, gifting, and even inheritance. Buying/selling is only one form of transfer, so "right to transfer" is the most accurate term.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果我把資產租給別人，我還擁有私有產權嗎？

Q: If I rent my asset to someone else, do I still have private property rights?

A: 非常好的問題！你的理解完全正確。 當你出租資產時，你其實是將該資產的「獨享使用權」暫時交給了租客，藉此來行使你的「獨享收入權」（收取租金）。同時，你依然保留著該資產的「轉讓權」（即你仍可以將整個物業賣給其他人）。因此，你依然擁有該資產的私有產權。

A: Excellent question! Your understanding is perfectly correct. When you rent out an asset, you are actually temporarily handing over the "exclusive right to use" to the tenant, thereby exercising your "exclusive right to derive income" (collecting rent). At the same time, you still retain the "right to transfer" of the asset (meaning you can still sell the entire property to someone else). Therefore, you still possess the private property rights to the asset.
