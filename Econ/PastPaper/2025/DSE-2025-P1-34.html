貨幣與銀行：存款創造 (Money & Banking: Deposit Creation)

題目 The Question

細閱下列某銀行系統的資產負債表。

Study the following balance sheet of a banking system.

資產 Assets ($) 負債 Liabilities ($)
儲備 Reserves: 250
貸款 Loans: 750		 存款 Deposits: 1000

原初，該銀行系統有 $50 的超額儲備。假設公眾決定將 $100 現金存入該銀行系統以減持現金。當該銀行系統達至最大存款時，下列哪項是其新的資產負債表？

Initially, the banking system has an excess reserve of $50. Suppose the public decides to reduce cash holdings by depositing $100 cash into the banking system. When the maximum deposit of the banking system is reached, which of the following will be its new balance sheet?

選項 Options: (請參考圖片中的 A, B, C, D 表格 / Please refer to tables A, B, C, D in the image)

概念重溫 Concept Review

要解決此題，必須掌握以下幾個核心概念：

To solve this problem, you must master the following core concepts:

  1. 法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio, RRR)
    這是銀行必須保留的存款百分比。我們可以透過原初狀態計算出來。 This is the percentage of deposits that banks must hold. We can calculate this from the initial state.
    $$ \text{RRR} = \frac{\text{法定儲備 (Required Reserves)}}{\text{存款 (Deposits)}} $$
  2. 最大存款額 (Maximum Deposits)
    當銀行借出所有超額儲備（即沒有超額儲備）時，存款達到最大值。 Deposits reach their maximum when banks lend out all excess reserves (i.e., zero excess reserves).
    $$ \text{Max. Deposits} = \frac{\text{總儲備 (Total Reserves)}}{\text{RRR}} $$
  3. 資產負債表平衡 (Balance Sheet Identity)
    資產必須永遠等於負債。 Assets must always equal Liabilities.
    $$ \text{Reserves} + \text{Loans} = \text{Deposits} $$

答案與詳細分析 Answer & Analysis

正確答案 Correct Answer: D

步驟 1：計算法定儲備比率 (Calculate RRR)

原初實際儲備為 $250，題目指出超額儲備為 $50。

Initially, actual reserves are $250, and the question states excess reserves are $50.

$$ \text{法定儲備} = 250 - 50 = 200 $$ $$ \text{RRR} = \frac{200}{1000} = 0.2 \quad (20\%) $$

步驟 2：計算新的總儲備 (Calculate New Total Reserves)

公眾存入 $100 現金。這筆錢直接進入銀行的金庫，成為新的儲備。

The public deposits $100 cash. This money goes directly into the bank's vault, becoming new reserves.

$$ \text{新總儲備 (New Reserves)} = 250 + 100 = 350 $$

步驟 3：計算最大存款額 (Calculate Max Deposits)

題目要求「達至最大存款」(Maximum deposit is reached)，這意味著銀行會將所有超額儲備借出，直到實際儲備剛好等於法定儲備。

The question asks for the state when "maximum deposit is reached", meaning banks lend out all excess reserves until actual reserves equal required reserves.

$$ \text{新存款 (New Deposits)} = \frac{\text{新總儲備 (350)}}{\text{RRR (0.2)}} = 1750 $$

步驟 4：計算貸款額 (Calculate Loans)

利用資產負債表平衡公式：

Using the balance sheet identity:

$$ \text{Loans} = \text{Deposits} - \text{Reserves} $$ $$ \text{Loans} = 1750 - 350 = 1400 $$

結論： 新的資產負債表應顯示：儲備 $350，貸款 $1400，存款 $1750。這與 選項 D 吻合。

Conclusion: The new balance sheet should show: Reserves $350, Loans $1400, Deposits $1750. This matches Option D.

題目拆解與技巧 Problem Decomposition & Tips

1. 找出隱藏變數 (Find the Hidden Variable):
題目很少直接給你 法定儲備比率 \(RRR\)。第一步永遠是利用「原初」狀態和「超額儲備」的數據來計算它。

The question rarely gives you the Required Reserve Ratio \(RRR\) directly. The first step is always to calculate it using the "Initial" state and "Excess Reserve" data.

2. 分清「現金」與「儲備」 (Distinguish Cash vs. Reserves):
當公眾存入現金，這筆錢全數變成銀行的儲備。不要先扣除法定儲備才加進去。先加總數，再算擴張。

When the public deposits cash, the entire amount becomes bank reserves. Do not deduct required reserves before adding it. Add the total first, then calculate expansion.

3. 檢查「最大」一詞 (Check for "Maximum"):
如果題目沒有說「最大存款」，答案可能是介於中間的任何數字。但一旦看到「最大 (Maximum)」，必須確保 \( \text{Total Reserves} = \text{Deposits} \times RRR \)。

If the question didn't say "maximum deposit", the answer could be any number in between. But once you see "Maximum", you must ensure \( \text{Total Reserves} = \text{Deposits} \times RRR \).
