HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: National Income (國民收入) Difficulty: ★★★★☆ (Level 4) Key Concepts: GDP, Imports, Retail Margin
The Question (題目)
假設某經濟減少在進口貨物方面的消費開支共達 10 百萬元，而這筆款項改而消費在全部由該經濟常住生產單位所生產的貨品上。這兩種改變會令該經濟的本地生產總值： Suppose an economy reduces its consumption expenditure on imported goods by $10 million and the whole amount is instead spent on goods wholly produced by its resident producing units. Because of these two changes, the GDP of this economy will:
  • A. 維持不變。 A. remain unchanged.
  • B. 增加 10 百萬元。 B. increase by $10 million.
  • C. 增加多於 10 百萬元。 C. increase by more than $10 million.
  • D. 增加少於 10 百萬元。 D. increase by less than $10 million.
Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. GDP Expenditure Approach (支出面計算法)

$$ GDP = C + I + G + (X - M) $$
C (私人消費開支): 包括所有最終產品的消費，不論產地來源。 C (Private Consumption Expenditure): Includes consumption of all final goods, regardless of their origin. M (進口): 為了計算本地生產總值，我們必須扣除進口成分，因為這些價值不是由本地常住生產單位創造的。 M (Imports): To calculate GDP, we must subtract the import content because this value was not created by resident producing units.

2. Retail Price vs Import Price (零售價與進口價)

消費開支是按市價 (Market Price) 計算的，這包含了進口成本加上本地的零售服務增值。 Consumption expenditure is calculated at Market Price, which includes the import cost plus the local retail service value added.
Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
Correct Option: D (增加少於 10 百萬元)

這道題目可以通過兩種不同的經濟邏輯來分析。兩者都會得出相同的結論。 This question can be analyzed using two different economic logics. Both lead to the same conclusion.

Method 1: Value Added Approach (價值增值法)
核心邏輯： 「消費開支」 (10M) 並不完全等於「進口值」。 Core Logic: "Consumption Expenditure" ($10M) is not equal to "Import Value".
$$ \text{Retail Price} = \text{Import Cost} + \text{Local Value Added (Margin)} $$ $$ 10 = V_{import} + V_{local} $$
舊情況 (Old Scenario): 買進口貨。 Old Scenario: Buying imported goods.
  • GDP 貢獻 = 本地零售商的服務價值 ($V_{local}$)。
  • Contribution to GDP = Value of local retail services ($V_{local}$).
  • 注意：進口成本 ($V_{import}$) 會被扣除。
  • Note: Import cost ($V_{import}$) is subtracted.
新情況 (New Scenario): 買本地貨。 New Scenario: Buying local goods.
  • GDP 貢獻 = 10 (因為全部由本地生產)。
  • Contribution to GDP = 10 (Since wholly produced locally).
GDP 變動 (Change):
$$ \Delta GDP = \text{New} - \text{Old} $$ $$ \Delta GDP = 10 - V_{local} $$
由於 $10 = V_{import} + V_{local}$，所以 $10 - V_{local} = V_{import}$。 因為進口成本必定小於零售價 ($V_{import} < 10$)，所以 GDP 增加少於 10。 Since $10 = V_{import} + V_{local}$, then $10 - V_{local} = V_{import}$. Because Import Cost must be less than Retail Price ($V_{import} < 10$), the GDP increase is less than 10.
Method 2: Accounting Identity Approach (會計恆等式法)
核心邏輯： 使用 $GDP = C + I - M$ 公式。 Core Logic: Using the $GDP = C + I - M$ formula. 原本情況 (Original): 消費者花 10M 買進口貨。 Original: Consumers spend 10M on imports.
  • $C \uparrow 10$
  • 但這貨品來源有兩個可能：The good came from two possible sources:
    1. 本年進口： $M \uparrow \text{Cost}$
    2. 存貨提取： $I \downarrow \text{Cost}$ (減存貨視為負投資)
  • 無論哪種情況，都要扣除成本 (Cost)。
  • In either case, the Cost is subtracted.
  • Old Contribution = $10 - \text{Cost}$
新情況 (New): 消費者花 10M 買本地貨。 New: Consumers spend 10M on local goods.
  • $C \uparrow 10$
  • 沒有進口漏損 ($M=0$)，也沒有存貨減少。
  • No import leakage ($M=0$), no inventory reduction.
  • New Contribution = $10$
結論 (Conclusion):
$$ \Delta GDP = 10 - (10 - \text{Cost}) = \text{Cost} $$
增加量等於該貨品的進口成本。由於成本必定小於售價 (10)，故選 D。 The increase equals the import cost of the good. Since Cost must be less than Price (10), choose D.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

Trap: Confusing Spending with Import Value (混淆消費開支與進口值)

錯誤觀念： 學生常以為「在進口貨消費 10M」等於「進口值 (M) 增加了 10M」。 Misconception: Students often think "Spending $10M on imports" means "Import Value (M) increases by $10M".

正確觀念： 消費開支 (C) 是 10M，但進口值 (M) 只是當中的成本部分（例如 6M）。剩下的 4M 是本地零售商賺取的，已經計入當年的 GDP。 Correction: Consumption (C) is $10M, but Import Value (M) is only the cost portion (e.g., $6M). The remaining $4M is earned by local retailers and is already counted in GDP.
Exam Tricks (考試技巧)
  • 極端例子思考法 (Extreme Case Thinking)： Extreme Case Thinking:
    試想像如果進口貨的成本是 0 元（雖然不可能，但有助思考）。那麼原本買進口貨就等於全數貢獻本地 GDP (100% 利潤)。轉買本地貨就沒有分別。
    試想像進口貨成本是 9.9 元。原本只貢獻 0.1 元。轉買本地貨貢獻 10 元。GDP 增加了 9.9 元（即是省回來的進口成本）。
