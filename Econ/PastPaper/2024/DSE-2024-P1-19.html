Topic: Efficiency & Market Failure (效率與市場失效) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Overproduction, Excess Supply, MSC vs MSB
下列哪項／些關於過度生產的陳述是正確的？
Which of the following statements about overproduction is/are correct?
(1) 當某物品有超額供應時，該物品會出現過度生產。
Overproduction of a good occurs when there is an excess supply of the good.

(2) 每當市場處於均衡狀態時，便不會出現過度生產。
Overproduction does not occur whenever the market is at equilibrium.

(3) 當過度生產出現時，最後一單位的邊際社會成本大於邊際社會利益。
When overproduction occurs, the marginal social cost is greater than the marginal social benefit at the last unit.
  • A. 只有 (2) (2) only
  • B. 只有 (3) (3) only
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3) (2) and (3) only
  • D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) (1), (2) and (3)
概念重溫 Concept Review

1. 效率 (Efficiency): 當邊際社會利益等於邊際社會成本時，資源配置達到最優。

Allocative efficiency is achieved when Marginal Social Benefit (MSB) equals Marginal Social Cost (MSC).

$$ MSB = MSC $$

2. 過度生產 (Overproduction): 當生產數量 ($Q$) 超過有效率的數量 ($Q^*$)。

Overproduction occurs when the quantity produced ($Q$) exceeds the efficient quantity ($Q^*$).

$$ Q > Q^* \implies MSC > MSB $$

3. 超額供應 (Excess Supply): 這是一個市場不均衡的狀態，指在特定價格下，供應量大於需求量。

Excess Supply is a state of market disequilibrium where Quantity Supplied ($Q_s$) is greater than Quantity Demanded ($Q_d$) at a specific price.

$$ Q_s > Q_d \quad (\text{at Price } P) $$

答案與詳細分析 Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: B (只有 3 / 3 only)
錯誤 False 陳述 (1) Statement (1)

分析：混淆了「超額供應」與「過度生產」。

「超額供應」是指價格高於均衡價格，導致賣家想賣的數量多於買家想買的數量 ($Q_s > Q_d$)，這是市場不均衡。而「過度生產」是指實際交易量超過了社會最優數量 ($Q > Q^*$)，這是資源配置缺乏效率。兩者沒有必然關係。即使市場有超額供應，實際交易量 ($Q_d$) 可能仍低於社會最優水平。

Analysis: Confuses "Excess Supply" with "Overproduction".

"Excess Supply" means the price is above equilibrium, causing Quantity Supplied to exceed Quantity Demanded ($Q_s > Q_d$). This is market disequilibrium. "Overproduction" means the actual quantity produced exceeds the socially optimal level ($Q > Q^*$). These are different concepts. Even with excess supply, the actual traded quantity ($Q_d$) could still be below the socially optimal level.

錯誤 False 陳述 (2) Statement (2)

分析：忽略了「界外效應」(Externalities)。

市場均衡只代表私人邊際利益等於私人邊際成本 ($MPB = MPC$)。如果存在生產的負界外效應 (例如污染)，社會成本會高於私人成本 ($MSC > MPC$)。此時，雖然市場處於均衡 ($MPB = MPC$)，但社會角度看卻是 $MSC > MSB$，導致過度生產。因此，市場均衡時可以出現過度生產。

Analysis: Ignores "Externalities".

Market equilibrium only implies Marginal Private Benefit equals Marginal Private Cost ($MPB = MPC$). If there are negative production externalities (e.g., pollution), the Social Cost is higher than the Private Cost ($MSC > MPC$). At market equilibrium, we would have $MSC > MSB$, resulting in overproduction. Therefore, overproduction can occur even when the market is at equilibrium.

正確 True 陳述 (3) Statement (3)

分析：符合過度生產的定義。

效率點在於 $MSC = MSB$。如果生產量過多 (過度生產)，意味著我們使用了價值較高的資源 ($MSC$) 去生產價值較低的產品 ($MSB$)。因此，在最後一個單位，邊際社會成本必定大於邊際社會利益 ($MSC > MSB$)。

Analysis: Matches the definition of overproduction.

The efficiency point is where $MSC = MSB$. If quantity is too high (overproduction), it means we are using high-value resources ($MSC$) to produce low-value goods ($MSB$). Therefore, at the last unit, Marginal Social Cost must be greater than Marginal Social Benefit ($MSC > MSB$).

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

關鍵點 (Pivot Point)：區分「市場均衡」(Market Equilibrium) 與 「社會效率」(Social Efficiency)。

  • 市場均衡：買賣雙方滿意 ($Q_d = Q_s$)，沒有改變價格的動力。
  • 社會效率：社會總盈餘最大化 ($MSC = MSB$)。

解題邏輯：

  1. 看到 (1)，問自己：貨倉堆積貨物 (超額供應) 是否等於社會浪費資源生產了沒人要的東西？不一定，這通常是價格機制問題。
  2. 看到 (2)，問自己：自由市場 (Invisible Hand) 是否完美？不是，如果有污染 (負界外效應)，市場均衡就會導致過度生產。
  3. 看到 (3)，畫圖或回想公式：$Q > Q^*$ 時，成本線 ($MSC$) 是否高於利益線 ($MSB$)？是的。

Pivot Point: Distinguish between "Market Equilibrium" and "Social Efficiency".

  • Market Equilibrium: Buyers and sellers are satisfied ($Q_d = Q_s$), no tendency for price to change.
  • Social Efficiency: Total social surplus is maximized ($MSC = MSB$).
常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes

陷阱：誤以為「市場均衡 = 有效率」。

這是經濟學初學者最大的誤解。只有在沒有界外效應的情況下，市場均衡才等於有效率。一旦有界外效應，市場均衡就會導致無效率 (過度生產或生產不足)。

Trap: Assuming "Market Equilibrium = Efficient".

This is the biggest misconception. Market equilibrium equals efficiency ONLY when there are no externalities. Once externalities exist, market equilibrium leads to inefficiency (overproduction or underproduction).
