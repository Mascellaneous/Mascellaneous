題目分析 Question Analysis

題目 Question:

下表顯示某國有關國際收支平衡的資料：

The following table shows the information about the balance of payments of a country:
項目 Item 百萬元 ($ million)
有形貿易 Visible trade 20
無形貿易 Invisible trade X
資本流入 Capital inflow 70
資本流出 Capital outflow 50

假設該國的外匯儲備上升了30百萬元，X 的值是多少？

Suppose the foreign exchange reserves of the country increase by $30 million. What is the value of X?

A. -70
B. -10
C. +10
D. +70

概念重溫 Concept Review

1. 國際收支平衡 (Balance of Payments, BoP)

國際收支平衡紀錄了一個國家在特定時期內與世界其他地區的所有經濟交易。主要由經常帳 (Current Account) 和資本及金融帳 (Capital and Financial Account) 組成。

The Balance of Payments records all economic transactions between a country and the rest of the world over a specific period. It mainly consists of the Current Account and the Capital and Financial Account.

2. 國際收支盈餘與儲備的關係 (Relationship between BoP Surplus and Reserves)

在香港中學文憑試 (HKDSE) 的課程架構下，國際收支差額等於官方儲備資產的變動：

Under the HKDSE curriculum framework, the Balance of Payments is equal to the change in official reserve assets:
$$ \text{國際收支差額} = \text{經常帳差額} + \text{資本及金融帳差額} $$ $$ \text{BoP Balance} = \text{Current Account Balance} + \text{Capital & Financial Account Balance} $$

關鍵邏輯：

  • 國際收支盈餘 (BoP Surplus) \(\rightarrow\) 外匯儲備上升 (Reserves Increase)
  • 國際收支赤字 (BoP Deficit) \(\rightarrow\) 外匯儲備下跌 (Reserves Decrease)
Key Logic: A BoP Surplus leads to an increase in reserves, while a BoP Deficit leads to a decrease in reserves.

答案詳解 The Answer

正確答案 Correct Option: B (-10)

步驟 1：計算經常帳差額 (Calculate Current Account Balance)

經常帳包含有形貿易和無形貿易。

The Current Account includes visible trade and invisible trade.
$$ \text{經常帳} = 20 + X $$

步驟 2：計算資本及金融帳差額 (Calculate Capital & Financial Account Balance)

這是資本流入減去資本流出的淨值。

This is the net value of capital inflow minus capital outflow.
$$ \text{資本及金融帳} = 70 - 50 = +20 $$

步驟 3：建立方程式 (Set up the equation)

題目指出外匯儲備上升了 30 百萬元，這意味著國際收支有 30 百萬元的盈餘。

The question states that foreign exchange reserves increased by $30 million, which means there is a Balance of Payments surplus of $30 million.
$$ \text{經常帳} + \text{資本及金融帳} = \text{儲備變動} $$ $$ (20 + X) + 20 = 30 $$

步驟 4：求解 X (Solve for X)

$$ 40 + X = 30 $$ $$ X = 30 - 40 $$ $$ X = -10 $$

因此，無形貿易差額為 -10 百萬元（即赤字）。

Therefore, the invisible trade balance is -$10 million (a deficit).

選項 A (-70)

這是錯誤的。這可能源於完全忽略了有形貿易和資本帳，或者計算符號嚴重錯誤。

Incorrect. This may result from completely ignoring visible trade and the capital account, or severe sign errors in calculation.

選項 C (+10)

這是錯誤的。如果你誤以為「儲備上升」代表在會計恆等式中需要減去（即 \( \text{BoP} + \text{Reserves} = 0 \) 的概念混淆），或者在移項時出錯 \( 40 - 30 = 10 \)，就會選錯。

Incorrect. You might choose this if you confuse the accounting identity (thinking BoP + Reserves = 0 implies a negative entry) or make a transposition error like \( 40 - 30 = 10 \).

選項 D (+70)

這是錯誤的。這可能是將所有數值相加而沒有考慮方程式的平衡。

Incorrect. This might happen if all values are simply added together without considering the balance of the equation.

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解題關鍵 Strategy:

  1. 識別淨值 (Identify Net Values):
    題目給出了資本流入和流出，第一步必須先計算「資本淨流入」 \( (70 - 50 = +20) \)。 The question gives capital inflow and outflow separately. The first step is to calculate the "Net Capital Inflow" \( (70 - 50 = +20) \).
  2. 理解儲備變動的符號 (Sign of Reserve Change):
    這是最常見的陷阱。在計算國際收支差額時，儲備上升 = 盈餘 (+)。
    如果題目問的是會計記帳（Balance of Payments Accounting），儲備上升會記為借方（Debit），但在計算 \( X \) 的數值題中，直接使用「收支差額 = 儲備變動」最為直觀。 This is the most common trap. When calculating the BoP balance, an increase in reserves = Surplus (+). While in BoP accounting, an increase is a debit, for numerical problems solving for \( X \), using "BoP Balance = Change in Reserves" is most intuitive.
  3. 代數運算 (Algebraic Manipulation):
    不要忘記有形貿易的 \( 20 \) 是正數。方程式應為 \( 20 + X + 20 = 30 \)，而不是 \( X + 20 = 30 \)。 Don't forget the visible trade of 20 is positive. The equation should be \( 20 + X + 20 = 30 \), not \( X + 20 = 30 \).
