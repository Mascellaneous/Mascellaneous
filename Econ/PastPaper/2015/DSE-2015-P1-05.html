DSE 2015 Paper 1 Q5 Analysis

題目 (The Question)

下表顯示在完全競爭市場內某廠商的邊際成本。

The schedule below shows the marginal cost of a firm in a perfectly competitive market.
產量 (單位) / Output (units) 3456789
邊際成本 ($) / Marginal Cost ($) 55.566.5789.5

假設租用機器的租金（屬固定成本）上升了 $2，而每單位產出的邊際成本減少了 $0.5。該廠商利潤極大化的產量因此__________，而利潤__________。

Suppose the rental of the machine hired, a fixed cost, increases by $2 while the marginal cost for each unit of the output is reduced by $0.5. As a result, the profit-maximising output of the firm __________ while the profit of the firm __________.
  • A. 上升 … 下跌 (increases … decreases)
  • B. 上升 … 可能上升或下跌 (increases … may increase or decrease)
  • C. 下跌 … 下跌 (decreases … decreases)
  • D. 下跌 … 可能上升或下跌 (decreases … may increase or decrease)
概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 利潤極大化條件 (Profit Maximization Condition):
在完全競爭市場中，廠商是價格接受者。利潤極大化的條件是： In a perfectly competitive market, the firm is a price taker. The condition for profit maximization is:

$$ MR = MC = P $$

若 \( MR > MC \)，廠商應增加產量以增加利潤。
若 \( MR < MC \)，廠商應減少產量以減少虧損。

If \( MR > MC \), the firm should increase output to increase profit. If \( MR < MC \), the firm should decrease output to reduce loss.

2. 固定成本與邊際成本 (Fixed Cost vs. Marginal Cost):
固定成本 (TFC) 的改變不會影響邊際成本 (MC)，因為 MC 只反映生產多一單位所需的額外成本。 Changes in Total Fixed Cost (TFC) do not affect Marginal Cost (MC), as MC only reflects the additional cost of producing one more unit.

正確答案 (Correct Answer): B
步驟一：分析產量的改變 (Step 1: Analyze the change in Output)

題目指出每單位的邊際成本減少了 $0.5。這代表整條 MC 曲線向下移動（或向右移動）。 The question states that the marginal cost for each unit is reduced by $0.5. This means the entire MC curve shifts downwards (or to the right).

在完全競爭市場，價格 \( P \)（即 \( MR \)）由市場決定，保持不變。當 MC 下跌時，在原本的產量水平上，我們會發現： In perfect competition, Price \( P \) (which is \( MR \)) is determined by the market and remains constant. When MC falls, at the original output level, we find:

$$ P > New\ MC $$

根據利潤極大化原則，當 \( MR > MC \) 時，廠商應該增加產量直到兩者再次相等。因此，產量上升。 According to the profit maximization principle, when \( MR > MC \), the firm should increase output until they are equal again. Therefore, output increases.

結論：排除選項 C 和 D。 (Conclusion: Eliminate options C and D.)

步驟二：分析利潤的改變 (Step 2: Analyze the change in Profit)

利潤的改變受兩個因素影響： The change in profit is affected by two factors:

  1. 固定成本上升 (Increase in Fixed Cost):
    租金上升 $2。這會直接令總成本上升 $2，對利潤有負面影響（利潤 -$2）。 Rent increases by $2. This directly increases Total Cost by $2, having a negative effect on profit (Profit -$2).
  2. 邊際成本下降 (Decrease in Marginal Cost):
    每單位 MC 下降 $0.5，這意味著平均可變成本 (AVC) 也下降 $0.5。總可變成本 (TVC) 節省了 \( 0.5 \times Q \)。這對利潤有正面影響（利潤 +$0.5Q$）。 MC decreases by $0.5 per unit, implying Average Variable Cost (AVC) also drops by $0.5. Total Variable Cost (TVC) is saved by \( 0.5 \times Q \). This has a positive effect on profit (Profit +$0.5Q$).

淨效果 (Net Effect):
利潤改變 = 成本節省 - 租金增加 Change in Profit = Cost Savings - Rent Increase

$$ \Delta Profit \approx (0.5 \times Q) - 2 $$

由於題目沒有提供市場價格 \( P \)，我們不知道廠商的均衡產量 \( Q \) 是多少。 Since the question does not provide the market price \( P \), we do not know the firm's equilibrium output \( Q \).

  • 如果產量 \( Q = 3 \)，節省 = \( 3 \times 0.5 = 1.5 \)。因為 \( 1.5 < 2 \)，利潤下跌。
    • If output \( Q = 3 \), savings = \( 3 \times 0.5 = 1.5 \). Since \( 1.5 < 2 \), profit decreases.
  • 如果產量 \( Q = 8 \)，節省 = \( 8 \times 0.5 = 4 \)。因為 \( 4 > 2 \)，利潤上升。
    • If output \( Q = 8 \), savings = \( 8 \times 0.5 = 4 \). Since \( 4 > 2 \), profit increases.

因此，利潤可能上升或下跌，視乎產量水平而定。 Therefore, profit may increase or decrease, depending on the output level.

題目拆解與陷阱 (Problem Decomposition & Traps)

1. 分開處理固定成本與變動成本 (Separate Fixed and Variable Costs):
學生常犯錯誤是以為固定成本上升會推高 MC。必須記住：固定成本 (Fixed Cost) 不影響 利潤極大化的產量決定 (MC 不變)，它只影響最終利潤的數值。 A common mistake is thinking fixed cost increases push up MC. Remember: Fixed Cost does not affect the profit-maximizing output decision (MC stays same); it only affects the final profit figure.

2. 未知數 \( P \) 與 \( Q \) (Unknown \( P \) and \( Q \)):
這類題目如果沒有給出具體的市場價格，我們就不能假設產量是某個特定數字。當變數（產量）未知時，涉及總量（如總成本節省）的比較通常會導致「不確定」的結論。 In questions where the market price is not given, we cannot assume output is a specific number. When a variable (output) is unknown, comparisons involving totals (like total cost savings) often lead to an "indeterminate" conclusion.
