Balance on services ($ million)

根據上述數據，可得出的結論是：

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 貿易差額 (Trade Balance)

物品差額（有形貿易差額）與服務差額（無形貿易差額）的計算方法相同：

Balance on goods (visible trade balance) and Balance on services (invisible trade balance) are calculated in the same way:

$$ \text{Balance} = \text{Exports} - \text{Imports} $$ $$ \text{差額} = \text{出口} - \text{進口} $$

若差額為正數 (+)，表示順差 (Surplus)；若差額為負數 (-)，表示逆差 (Deficit)。

A positive (+) balance indicates a surplus; a negative (-) balance indicates a deficit.

2. 經常帳 (Current Account)

經常帳主要包括：物品差額 + 服務差額 + 要素收益淨流動 + 經常轉移淨值。

Current Account mainly includes: Balance on goods + Balance on services + Net factor income from abroad + Net current transfers.

注意：題目只提供了物品和服務差額，我們無法得知經常帳的完整數據。

Note: The question only provides balances on goods and services. We do not have full data for the Current Account.

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確選項 (Correct Option): D

錯誤 Wrong 選項 A (Option A)

分析：我們不能單憑差額的改善來斷定出口增加。

物品差額由 2020 年的 -41,135 變為 2021 年的 24,671。這表示 $E - M$ 的數值變大了，但這不一定是因為 $E$ (出口) 變大。

Analysis: We cannot conclude that exports increased solely based on the improvement in the balance.

The balance on goods improved from -41,135 in 2020 to 24,671 in 2021. This means the value of $E - M$ increased, but this is not necessarily because $E$ (Exports) increased.

反證例子 (Counter-example):

假設 2020 年：
$$ E_{2020} = 100,000, \quad M_{2020} = 141,135 $$ $$ \text{Balance} = 100,000 - 141,135 = -41,135 $$

假設 2021 年 (出口保持不變，進口大幅下跌)：
$$ E_{2021} = 100,000, \quad M_{2021} = 75,329 $$ $$ \text{Balance} = 100,000 - 75,329 = 24,671 $$

在這個情況下，差額雖然改善了，但出口並沒有增加 ($E_{2021} = E_{2020}$)。因此選項 A 不一定正確。

In this scenario, although the balance improved, exports did not increase ($E_{2021} = E_{2020}$). Therefore, Option A is not necessarily true.

錯誤 Wrong 選項 B (Option B)

分析：經常帳不僅包括物品和服務差額，還包括「要素收益流動」和「經常轉移」。

雖然 2020 年的物品和服務總差額為 $-41,135 + 92,948 = +51,813$ 百萬元，但若該年的「要素收益淨流出」極大（例如大於 51,813），經常帳仍可能是逆差。資料不足，無法下定論。

Analysis: The Current Account includes not only goods and services balances but also "Factor income flows" and "Current transfers".

Although the combined balance of goods and services in 2020 is $-41,135 + 92,948 = +51,813$ million, if the "Net factor income outflow" was huge (e.g., greater than 51,813), the Current Account could still be in deficit. Insufficient data to conclude.

錯誤 Wrong 選項 C (Option C)

分析：國際收支平衡表包括經常帳、資本及金融帳。單憑貿易數據無法得知整體的國際收支狀況。

即使貿易有順差，若金融帳有巨額資金外流，整體國際收支仍可能是赤字。資料不足。

Analysis: The Balance of Payments includes the Current Account and the Capital and Financial Account. Trade data alone cannot determine the overall BOP position.

Even with a trade surplus, if there is a massive capital outflow in the financial account, the overall BOP could still be in deficit. Insufficient data.

正確 Correct 選項 D (Option D)

分析：這是一個數學邏輯推論。

公式：$$ \text{服務差額} = \text{服務出口} - \text{服務進口} $$

題目數據顯示：
2020年服務差額 = 92,948 > 0
2021年服務差額 = 135,042 > 0

既然兩年的服務差額都是正數 (Positive)，這必然意味著在這兩年中：服務出口 > 服務進口。

Analysis: This is a mathematical logical deduction.

Formula: $$ \text{Balance on services} = \text{Exports of services} - \text{Imports of services} $$

The data shows:
2020 Balance on services = 92,948 > 0
2021 Balance on services = 135,042 > 0

Since the balance on services is positive in both years, it necessarily implies that in both years: Exports of services > Imports of services.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

關鍵點 (Pivot Point)：分辨「差額」(Net Balance) 與「總值」(Gross Value) 的區別。

Pivot Point: Distinguish between "Net Balance" and "Gross Value".

解題步驟 (Steps)：

  1. 檢查數據性質：題目只給了「差額」(Balance)，即 $E - M$ 的結果。
    Check Data Nature: The question only provides "Balance", which is the result of $E - M$.
  2. 代數思維 (Algebraic Thinking)：對於選項 A，問自己：如果 $E - M$ 變大，是否代表 $E$ 一定變大？(答案是否定的，因為 $M$ 變小也可以)。
    Algebraic Thinking: For Option A, ask yourself: If $E - M$ increases, does it imply $E$ must increase? (The answer is no, because $M$ decreasing also works).
  3. 排除「資料不足」選項：題目只給了貿易數據，任何關於「經常帳」(B) 或「國際收支」(C) 的結論都因為缺乏其他組成部分數據而無法成立。
    Eliminate "Insufficient Data" Options: The question only gives trade data. Any conclusion about "Current Account" (B) or "Balance of Payments" (C) is invalid due to missing data on other components.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

1. 混淆差額變動與總值變動：

學生常誤以為差額由負轉正（或數值變大）一定代表出口表現好轉。其實，經濟衰退導致進口需求大幅下降，也會令差額數值「改善」。

Confusing change in balance with change in gross value: Students often mistakenly think an improvement in balance (or turning from negative to positive) implies better export performance. In reality, an economic recession causing a sharp drop in import demand can also "improve" the balance figure.

2. 忽略經常帳的其他組成部分：

看到物品和服務加起來是正數，就以為經常帳是順差，忘記了還有「要素收益」和「轉移」可能導致逆差。

Ignoring other components of the Current Account: Seeing that goods and services combined are positive, students assume the Current Account is in surplus, forgetting that "Factor Income" and "Transfers" could cause a deficit.
