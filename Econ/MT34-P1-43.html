HKDSE Economics Tutor: Question Analysis

Topic: Market Intervention & International Trade (市場干預與國際貿易) Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Key Concepts: Tariff (關稅), Import (進口), Total Expenditure (總支出)

The Question (題目)

[中文] 下圖顯示某小型開放經濟中物品 X 的市場。政府對物品 X 徵收進口關稅。
假設物品 X 的國際價格上升，同時物品 X 的需求上升，下列哪（幾）項是正確的？

  1. 本地消費者對物品 X 的總支出會上升。
  2. 政府從物品 X 得到的關稅收入可能上升、下降或維持不變。
  3. 物品 X 的本地生產量會上升。

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)、(3)

[English] The figure below shows the market for Good X in a small open economy. The government imposes an import tariff on Good X.
Suppose the world price of Good X increases, and simultaneously the demand for Good X increases. Which of the following is/are correct?

  1. Total expenditure by local consumers on Good X will increase.
  2. Tariff revenue received by the government from Good X may increase, decrease, or remain unchanged.
  3. The local production volume of Good X will increase.

A. (1), (2) only
B. (1), (3) only
C. (2), (3) only
D. (1), (2), (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

在小型開放經濟中，實施關稅後，本地市場的價格（\(P_d\)）由國際價格（\(P_w\)）加上單位關稅（\(t\)）決定。

In a small open economy, after imposing a tariff, the domestic market price (\(P_d\)) is determined by the world price (\(P_w\)) plus the per-unit tariff (\(t\)).
$$ P_d = P_w + t $$ $$ \text{Import Volume (進口量, } M \text{)} = Q_d - Q_s $$ $$ \text{Tariff Revenue (關稅收入, } TR \text{)} = t \times M = t \times (Q_d - Q_s) $$ $$ \text{Total Consumer Expenditure (總支出, } TE \text{)} = P_d \times Q_d $$
  • 本地需求量 (\(Q_d\)): 取決於需求曲線 (\(D\)) 及本地價格 (\(P_d\))。
    Local quantity demanded depends on the demand curve and domestic price.
  • 本地供應量/生產量 (\(Q_s\)): 取決於供應曲線 (\(S\)) 及本地價格 (\(P_d\))。
    Local quantity supplied/production depends on the supply curve and domestic price.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option C

Illustration of Market Changes (市場變動圖解)

Statement (1) - False (錯誤)

題目稱： 本地消費者對物品 X 的總支出會上升。

分析： 總支出 \(TE = P_d \times Q_d\)。已知國際價格上升，導致本地價格上升 (\(P_d \uparrow\))。然而，本地需求量 (\(Q_d\)) 的變化是不確定的：一方面，價格上升會令 \(Q_d\) 沿著需求曲線下跌；另一方面，需求增加（曲線向右移）會令 \(Q_d\) 增加。如果價格上升的幅度極大，而需求只微增，新的 \(Q_d\) 可能會大幅下跌，導致總支出最終下跌。由於結果不確定，說它「會（必定）」上升是錯誤的。

Analysis: Total Expenditure \(TE = P_d \times Q_d\). The world price increases, causing domestic price to rise (\(P_d \uparrow\)). However, the change in quantity demanded (\(Q_d\)) is uncertain: the price increase causes a movement along the curve, decreasing \(Q_d\); meanwhile, the increase in demand (rightward shift) increases \(Q_d\). If the price hike is massive while the demand shift is small, \(Q_d\) could drop significantly, potentially causing \(TE\) to fall. Since the outcome is uncertain, stating it "will" definitely increase is incorrect.

Statement (2) - True (正確)

題目稱： 政府從物品 X 得到的關稅收入可能上升、下降或維持不變。

分析： 關稅收入 \(TR = t \times (Q_d - Q_s)\)。如在 (3) 中所述，\(Q_s\) 必定上升。而如在 (1) 中所述，\(Q_d\) 的變化是不確定的。既然 \(Q_s\) 上升而 \(Q_d\) 不確定，進口量 (\(Q_d - Q_s\)) 的變化自然也是不確定的。因此，關稅收入確實「可能上升、下降或維持不變」。這句陳述是絕對正確的。

Analysis: Tariff Revenue \(TR = t \times (Q_d - Q_s)\). As analyzed in (3), \(Q_s\) definitely increases. As analyzed in (1), the change in \(Q_d\) is uncertain. Since \(Q_s\) increases and \(Q_d\) is uncertain, the change in import volume (\(Q_d - Q_s\)) is also completely uncertain. Therefore, saying the revenue "may increase, decrease, or remain unchanged" accurately reflects this uncertainty and is a true statement.

Statement (3) - True (正確)

題目稱： 物品 X 的本地生產量會上升。

分析： 本地生產量 (\(Q_s\)) 由本地價格與供應曲線的相交點決定。因為物品的國際價格 (\(P_w\)) 上升，新的本地價格 (\(P_w + t\)) 也隨之上升。沿著原本向右上傾斜的供應曲線，價格上升必定導致本地生產量 (\(Q_s\)) 增加。

Analysis: Local production volume (\(Q_s\)) is determined by the intersection of the domestic price and the supply curve. Because the world price (\(P_w\)) increases, the new domestic price (\(P_w + t\)) is higher. Moving along the upward-sloping supply curve, a higher price strictly dictates that local production volume (\(Q_s\)) will increase.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵): DSE 經濟科經常考驗「雙重變動 (Simultaneous Shifts)」。當有兩個因素同時改變時，通常會有一個變數的方向是確定 (Certain) 的，而另一個變數的方向是不確定 (Uncertain) 的。

在這題中：
1. \(P \uparrow\) (導致 \(Q_d \downarrow, Q_s \uparrow\))
2. 需求 \(D \uparrow\) (導致 \(Q_d \uparrow\))

綜合兩者，\(Q_s\) 受到單一方向的刺激（必定上升），而 \(Q_d\) 受到一加一減的拉扯（變化不確定）。只要你看穿了「\(Q_d\) 不確定」這個核心，Statement (1) 和 (2) 的真偽便會原形畢露。

Pivot Point: The HKDSE Economics exam frequently tests "Simultaneous Shifts". When two factors change at once, usually one resulting variable is certain while another is uncertain. Here, \(P \uparrow\) pushes \(Q_d\) down and \(Q_s\) up, while \(D \uparrow\) pushes \(Q_d\) up. Combining these, \(Q_s\) is pushed in only one direction (definitely increases), whereas \(Q_d\) is pulled in opposite directions (uncertain change). Recognizing the uncertainty of \(Q_d\) is the key to judging statements (1) and (2).

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1: 為甚麼需求上升 (Demand increases) 不能抵消價格上升帶來的影響，確保總支出一定上升？

A: 經濟學中，我們不能假設「需求上升」的幅度一定很大。試想像，如果國際價格突然暴漲了 300%，但本地需求只因為輕微因素增加了 1%，本地消費者面對天價極可能會大幅減少購買量。在這種情況下，即便價格上升了，但數量 (\(Q_d\)) 跌得更厲害，總支出 (\(P \times Q_d\)) 最終還是會下跌的。

Q1: Why doesn't an increase in demand guarantee that total expenditure will increase, offsetting the price effect?
A: In economics, we cannot assume the magnitude of the "increase in demand". Imagine if the world price skyrocketed by 300%, but demand only increased by 1%. Facing astronomical prices, consumers would drastically cut their purchase volume. In this scenario, even though \(P\) is higher, \(Q_d\) falls so significantly that total expenditure (\(P \times Q_d\)) ends up decreasing.

Q2: Statement 2 裡面寫「可能上升、下降或維持不變」，這種看似廢話的選項通常是正確的嗎？

A: 在 DSE 的多項選擇題中，當題目涉及「不確定的變數」(如本題的進口量)，這類包含所有可能性的陳述 (May increase, decrease or remain unchanged) 幾乎必定是正確的陳述。因為它客觀地描述了「不確定性」這個事實。

Q2: Statement 2 says "may increase, decrease, or remain unchanged". Are these seemingly "say-nothing" options usually correct?
A: In HKDSE MCQs, when a question involves a variable with an uncertain outcome (like the import volume here), a statement encompassing all possibilities is almost always a factually correct statement. It accurately and objectively describes the reality of the mathematical uncertainty.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

Elimination Method (消去法):

  1. 第一眼看圖及題目：國際價格上升 \(\rightarrow\) 本地價格必定上升。這必然刺激本地供應，所以 (3) 必定正確。立即淘汰 A (因為沒有 3)。
  2. 再看 (2)：由於選項字眼包含「可能上升、下降或維持不變」(May... or remain unchanged)，在雙重變動 (Simultaneous shifts) 的題型中，這幾乎是必對的。如果你確認了這點，答案就只剩下 C (2, 3) 或 D (1, 2, 3)。
  3. 最後對付 (1)：「會」(Will) 是一個絕對字眼 (Absolute term)。在有不確定性 (\(Q_d\) 變動不明) 的情況下，帶有絕對字眼的結論通常是錯誤的。因此 (1) 錯，秒殺得 C。
Shortcut:
1. \(P_w\) rises \(\rightarrow\) Domestic price rises \(\rightarrow\) \(Q_s\) rises. So (3) is definitely correct. Cross out A.
2. Statement (2) uses "may...". In simultaneous shift questions, this broad phrasing is highly likely to be true. Now you are between C and D.
3. Statement (1) uses "will", an absolute term. Since \(Q_d\) change is uncertain, predicting \(TE\) "will" increase is a logical fallacy. Cross out (1). The answer is strictly C.
